Who is Zeni?

Zeni is a personal AI chatbot built by Arman as a passion project. It's designed to have natural conversations, answer your questions, and help with everyday things all inside a clean and simple interface.

Whether you want to chat freely, explore ideas, or just get quick answers, Zeni is here to help like a knowledgeable friend.

What can it do?
Chat Freely Have real conversations on any topic you like
Answer Questions Get clear answers to almost anything you ask
Image Analysis Upload any image and ask Zeni anything about it
Smart Context Remembers what was said earlier in the conversation

How it Works

Zeni runs on (Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite) Google's fast and capable AI model, accessed through OpenRouter under fair-use terms. The entire interface and experience is custom-built from scratch.

Privacy Notice
Please read this before using

Your messages are sent to an external AI model (Gemini via OpenRouter) to generate responses. Your chats do not reach us but the AI provider may use conversation data to improve their models. So please be careful about what you share.

  • Do not share passwords, OTPs, or login credentials
  • Do not share Aadhaar, PAN, or any ID numbers
  • Do not share bank details or financial information
  • Do not share any sensitive personal data
